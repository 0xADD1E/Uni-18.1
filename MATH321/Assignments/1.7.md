## 3
Show that the square of an even number is an even number using a direct proof.

---

Take an even number $p$ such that $p = 2k$

$p^2 = (2k)^2$

$p^2 = 4k^2 = 2(2k^2)$

As $p^2$ can be expressed in the form of $2(k)$, $p^2$ is even
